				ERP's (Enterprise Resource Planning) or Connectors
				
				
-- ERP is a software system that helps you run your entire business, supporting automation and processes in finance,  
   human resources,  manufacturing, supply chain, services, procurement, and more.
-- Major types of ERP's
The three most common types of ERP are on-premises, cloud-based, and hybrid ERP. An on-premises ERP system is run on-location on a company's servers, while cloud-based ERP operates on a third-party, remote server.
